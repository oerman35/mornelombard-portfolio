Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Round Trip Checklist ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

Round Trip Checklist is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Checklist content or data
  • Location information
  • Photos or media files
  • Contact information
  • Any personally identifiable information

Technical Information We May Collect

  • Device Operating System Type: We may collect basic information about whether you're using iOS, Android, or web version of our app for technical compatibility purposes only
  • App Store Analytics: We use standard analytics provided by Apple's App Store and Google's Play Store to understand general app performance and usage patterns

How Your Data is Stored

All checklist data you create is stored locally on your device only using iOS UserDefaults. This means:

  • Your checklists never leave your device
  • We cannot access your checklist content
  • Your data is not transmitted to any server or any third party
  • If you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties because we don't collect personal information in the first place.

Third-Party Services

Round Trip Checklist does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs that could access your data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from users of any age, including children under 13. Since all data remains on the user's device, there are no special privacy concerns for younger users.

Data Security

Since your data never leaves your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features and any passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID you have configured.

Future Changes

We may update our privacy practices in the future. If we plan to add features that involve data collection or transmission (such as cloud synchronization), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy before implementing such features
  • Clearly notify users of any changes
  • Ensure any new features remain optional and user-controlled

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal information on our servers, there is no personal data for us to modify or delete. You have complete control over your data since it remains on your device.

International Data Transfers

Since your data never leaves your device, there are no international data transfers.
